Why riders and drivers love CoGo

Built for Egypt's roads — simple, affordable, and safe.

Live trip tracking

Real-time GPS sharing so riders always know where the driver is. Native Apple Maps on iOS, Google Maps on Android.

Fair pricing

Transparent per-km rates. Riders save compared to taxis, drivers offset fuel costs. Everyone wins.

Verified community

Driver verification with license and vehicle checks. Ratings and reviews after every trip.

Easy booking

Find trips near you, book a seat in seconds, and get picked up at your location. No calls needed.

Arabic & English

Full RTL Arabic support and English — switch anytime. Built for Egyptian users first.

Reduce traffic

Fewer cars on the road means less congestion and lower emissions. Commute responsibly.

How it works

1

Post or find a trip

Drivers post their route, time, and available seats. Riders search by destination.

2

Book your seat

Request to join. The driver accepts and you get a confirmed pickup point.

3

Ride together

Track the driver in real-time. Get picked up, ride safely, and pay your fare.

4

Rate & repeat

Leave a review, build your reputation, and save on your next trip.
